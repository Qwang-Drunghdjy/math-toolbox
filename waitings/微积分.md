# 微积分

## 基本积分表

```math
\begin{align}
    & \int \mathrm{d}x = x + C \\
    & \int k\mathrm{d}x = kx + C, & k \neq 0 \\
    & \int x^{\mu}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{x^{\mu+1}}{\mu+1} + C, &\mu \neq -1 \\
    & \int \frac{1}{x}\mathrm{d}x = \ln |x| + C, & x \neq 0 \\
    & \int \frac{1}{1 + x^2}\mathrm{d}x = \arctan x + C \\
    & \int \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}}\mathrm{d}x = \arcsin{x} + C \\
    & \int \cos{x}\mathrm{d}x = \sin{x} + C \\
    & \int \sin{x}\mathrm{d}x = - \cos{x} + C \\
    & \int \frac{1}{\cos^2{x}}\mathrm{d}x = \int \sec^2{x}\mathrm{d}x = \tan{x} + C \\
    & \int \frac{1}{\sin^2{x}}\mathrm{d}x = \int \csc^2{x}\mathrm{d}x = - \cot{x} + C \\
    & \int \sec{x}\tan{x}\mathrm{d}x = \sec{x} + C \\
    & \int \csc{x}\cot{x}\mathrm{d}x = - \csc{x} + C \\
    & \int e^xdx = e^x + C \\
    & \int a^xdx = \frac{a^x}{\ln a} + C, & a > 0, a \neq 1 \\
    & \int \sh{x}\mathrm{d}x = \ch{x} + C \\
    & \int \ch{x}\mathrm{d}x = \sh{x} + C \\
\end{align}
```
